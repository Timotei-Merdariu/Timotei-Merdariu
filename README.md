## Projects

### TUCaN – Eurobot 2026 Robotics Competition (in progress)

Member of the TUCaN robotics team that qualified for the International Eurobot 2026 competition.  
The team is designing and building a fully autonomous robot from scratch according to the official Eurobot rules.

Project objectives:
- Detect and identify game objects ("crates") during the match
- Distinguish between two crate colors using onboard sensing
- Collect and transport crates to specific scoring zones
- Execute autonomous tasks within the competition time constraints

As a team we are working on – Electrical Team:
- Contributing to the electrical architecture of the robot
- Designing and reviewing the electrical schematic
- Integrating sensors, motor drivers, and communication interfaces
- Working on encoder signal acquisition from the drive motors
- Implementing communication between an Arduino Uno and a Raspberry Pi for reliable encoder reading and system coordination

Skills involved:
- Embedded systems integration
- Electrical system design
- Robotics system architecture
- Sensor interfacing
- Team-based engineering development
